About

Born in St. Petersburg Russia, and now living and working in Austin, Texas, Arkohva, or ARK, learned a lot growing up in the former Soviet Union. It was there that ARK developed their passion for socio-political commentary and street art. Often using paint to depict the plight of the oppressed and powerless, while pointing out hypocrisy in society, ARK chooses to remain anonymous because of the belief that the message is more important than the messenger. ARK's thought-provoking street art continued upon a big move to the American Midwest, to continue their education. After a few years spent in Milwaukee, ARK moved and settled in Austin, Texas. You can find many of Arkohva's work around Austin both in public view and in private collections.
